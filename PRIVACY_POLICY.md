# SafeCircle PK - Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 15, 2026**

SafeCircle PK ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Location Data
The core feature of SafeCircle PK is real-time location sharing within your family circle. We collect:
*   **Precise Location**: Your GPS coordinates to show your location to family members.
*   **Background Location**: We collect location data even when the app is closed or not in use to ensure continuous safety monitoring for your family.
*   **Safe Zones**: Locations you designate as "Safe Zones" to trigger alerts when you enter or exit.

### Device Information
We collect information about your device, including:
*   **Battery Level**: To inform your family if your phone is running out of power.
*   **Device Identifiers**: To uniquely identify your device for push notifications.

### User Account Information
*   **Phone Number**: Used for authentication via OTP.
*   **Display Name**: Used to identify you to your family members.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
*   To provide real-time location tracking for your family circle.
*   To send "Safe Zone" alerts to your family members.
*   To broadcast SOS alerts in case of emergencies.
*   To optimize battery and data usage (Pakistan-specific optimizations).

## 3. Data Sharing
We **do not** sell your data. Your location and status are shared **only** with members of the specific family circle(s) you have joined.

## 4. Background Location Disclosure
SafeCircle PK collects location data to enable real-time family tracking and geofencing alerts even when the app is closed or not in use. You can pause location sharing at any time from the Privacy Settings.

## 5. Data Security
We use Firebase (Google Cloud) for secure data storage and encryption.

## 6. Your Choices
*   **Pause Sharing**: You can stop sharing your location at any time.
*   **Invisible Mode**: You can hide your presence from the map.
*   **Delete Account**: You can leave your family circle and request data deletion.

## 7. Contact Us
For any privacy-related questions, please contact us within the app.
